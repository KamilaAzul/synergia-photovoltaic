About Us

Synergia is a family-run business, established in 2020 that prides itself on high standards of service, care, and customer experience.
I am an electrician and a certified renewable energy installer. I founded this company setting up photovoltaic installations due to my interest in renewable energy sources and work experience acquired in Germany and Switzerland, where I worked for many years.
Thanks to the fact that I personally make the valuation and connection of the installation, the client can influence the details select components that meet expectations and be sure that the installation is equipped with all security elements of the installation giving the user a good night's sleep.
Running a small family business has the advantage of direct contact between the ordering party and the contractor, where the client will not be sent to the hotline, and will certainly get answers to the questions he is interested in. In my work as an installer, the most important for me is the quality of workmanship and customer satisfaction.
